Gluten Free Portuguese Pizza

Description

Gluten free doesn't have to mean disgusting and dry pizza dough anymore. To make this pizza you can use the dough from this Brazilian Cheese Bread Recipe! PÃ£o de Queijo (Brazilian Cheese Bread) is naturally gluten free since it is made with tapioca flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 batch of Brazilian Cheese Bread
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 hard boiled eggs, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced black forest ham
  • 1/8 cup black olives
  • 1/4 onion, sliced thinly
  • 1 tsp of oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Roll the dough into two pizzas
  3. Bake dough for 12 minutes. It is okay if it isn't fully cooked.
  4. Spoon and spread the tomato sauce onto the half-baked pizza shells
  5. Sprinkle on the cheese
  6. Add ham, olives, onions and sliced eggs
  7. Bake 12 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
