Homemade Yogurt

Description

I totally free-styled this recipe after realizing that a small tub of yogurt was almost $10 at our local grocery store! I suppose the idea of fermenting dairy may turn people off who are not comfortable in the kitchen, but I promise you, making yogurt is SO easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 litre of homo milk
  • 2 tbsp of store-bought yogurt

Instructions

  1. Place the milk in a saucepan and heat to a boil while stirring so the milk doesn't burn.
  2. Once the mill begins to bubble up, remove the saucepan from the heat
  3. Leave on counter or in an ice bath until temperature reaches 110 - 115 degrees Fahrenheit
  4. Add the 2 tablespoons of yogurt into a large 1 litre mason jar (or other container)
  5. Add the milk and stir.
  6. Find a warm place to incubate the yogurt for 5 to 7 hours.
  7. Test after 5 hours to check on flavour. I prefer waiting 7 hours as I like my yogurt more tangy.
  8. Place in the fridge to cool and enjoy!
